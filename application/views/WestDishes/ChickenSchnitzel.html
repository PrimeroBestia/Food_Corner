CHICKEN SCHNITZEL

Western Dish (Germany)

Takes 35 minutes

Makes 4 servings

MAIN DISH

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken fillets
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 150g breadcrumbs
  • butter or cooking oil

Instruction

  • 1. Tenderise the chicken with a meat mallet.
  • 2. Season the flour with salt and pepper.
  • 3. Coat the chicken pieces in the flour.
  • 5. Beat the eggs.
  • 6. Coat the chicken in the egg mixture and place immediately into the breadcrumbs.
  • 7. Heat oil in a pan and fry the schnitzel on both sides until brown and the chicken is cooked through.
  • 8. Alternatively the schnitzel can be baked in an oven for 15-20 minutes at 180Â°C.
