Bulgogi (Korean Beef BBQ)

Eastern Dish (Korea)

Takes 20 minutes

Makes 5 serving

MAIN DISH

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef tenderloin, sliced thinly into pieces 1/2 inch x 2 inches and 1/8 inch thick
  • 1/2 cup of crushed pear
  • 1/4 cup onion puree
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1 chopped green onion
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs brown sugar (or 1 tbs of brown sugar and 1 1/2 tbs rice syrup)
  • a pinch of ground black pepper
  • 1 tbs toasted sesame oil
  • several thin slices of carrot

Instruction

  • 1. Mix all the marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  • 2. Add the sliced beef and mix well.
  • 3. You can grill, pan-fry, or BBQ right after marinating, but it's best to keep it in the fridge and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for a tougher cut of beef. Serve with ssamjang. (Ssamjang is a korean spicy dipping sauce)
